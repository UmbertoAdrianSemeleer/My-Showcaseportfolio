Project Description: Loyalties of Lucifer

Client Description

Client: Team Liquid

Team Liquid is a professional esports organization known for their innovative fan engagement and loyalty programs.

Project Overview

Loyalties of Lucifer was our third-semester project, lasting the entire semester. Our team of five created a loyalty program for Lucifer Coffee Roasters using NFC technology, in partnership with Team Liquid.

Assignment

Establish a media agency and develop a loyalty program similar to Liquid+ for a chosen company, aiming to enhance customer interaction and engagement.

Project Details

  • Client: Team Liquid
  • Company: Lucifer Coffee Roasters
  • Technology: NFC (Near Field Communication)
  • Objective: Improve customer interaction through a tailored loyalty program

Execution

We designed an NFC-based loyalty program to enhance customer experience at Lucifer Coffee Roasters. This program, inspired by Liquid+, aimed to foster customer loyalty and engagement by enabling quick reward redemption and tracking customer interactions.
